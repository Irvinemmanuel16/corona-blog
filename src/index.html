COVID 19

Also known as "coronavirus disease" is an infectious disease caused by a recently discovered coronavirus. Most people who get sick from COVID 19 experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.

Continue reading...

Symptoms

The most common symptoms are the following:
  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Fatigue
Other less common symptoms include the following:
  • Aches and pains
  • Diarrhea
  • Loss of sense of smell or taste
Serious symptoms are as follows:
  • Difficulty breathing or feeling short of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Inability to speak or move
